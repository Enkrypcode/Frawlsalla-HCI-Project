Frawlsalla About

A 2D platform fighter where the best of the baddest-ass warriors in history battle each other in an eternal tourney of champions for bragging rights, infinite mead, and the pure pleasure of delivering a beatdown. Includes online 4 players free for all, team games, custom games, and local play options. Upon its full release on February 20, 2021, Frawlsalla was available for Microsoft Windows, macOS, and Linux. On March 5, 2021, 2RSoft had acquired Frawlsalla. On October 6, 2021, Frawlsalla released a mobile port of the game for iOS and Android.




Frawlsalla Features : Gamemodes

FrawlMode

This mode can be played in many different matchmaking modes, such as Experimental 1v1, Friendly 2v2, and also Ranked 1v1 and 2v2. It is also typically the gamemode used in tournaments.In the FrawlMode gamemode, each player has a certain amount of lives.

Kungfoot

This mode can be played in Custom Online matches. In Kung Foot, two teams compete against each other on a stage with a net for each side and a large ball dropped in the middle. The main objective is to attack the ball and shoot it into your opponent's net to score points.

Frawlsketball

This mode can be played in Custom Online matches. In Frawlsketball, players are attempting to throw bombs at the opponents net (a small floating circle). Bombs will continually respawn on both sides and the middle of the stage. If a player hits the opposing team's net with a bomb, the will score a point.
